  • Look at circles: mouse over to see the correlation (-1 to 1) between statements about languages. The bigger and more solid the circle, the more correlated the statements. Red means strongly anti-correlated (value near -1), so languages typically match one of the statements, but not bother nor neither. Yellow and transparent means no correlation (value near 0).
